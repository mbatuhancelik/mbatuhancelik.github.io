**PaLM-E** is a pioneer framework that directly **incorporates continuous real-world sensor modalities into a large language model** to bridge the gap between words and perception. By embedding inputs like images and continuous state estimations directly into the language model's latent space as "multimodal sentences," it transforms a pre-trained LLM into an end-to-end embodied decision-maker. This design yields highly data-efficient sequential robot planning and exceptional zero-shot generalization while functioning simultaneously as a competitive vision-language generalist.

This paper was the spearhead of LLM-Robotics of the pre GPT4 era.
